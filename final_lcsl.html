About Us

Helping Children Learn Computers for Free

Learn Computer Sierra Leone is a non-governmental organization dedicated to bridging the digital divide in Sierra Leone. We provide free computer education to children in underserved communities, giving them the skills they need to succeed in our increasingly digital world.

Our programs focus on teaching fundamental computer literacy, and digital skills that empower children to become future leaders in technology and innovation as we work with both Primary and secondary schools.
